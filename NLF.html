NFL Quick Hits

The league handed out several major fines this week: Rashod Bateman was fined $25,154 for verbally abusing an official; Josh Allen and Elijah Moore were each fined $13,888 for excessive celebrations; George Pickens got $17,389 for unsportsmanlike conduct. Reuters Injury and availability updates ahead of Week 10: Brock Purdy of the San Francisco 49ers is getting closer to a return. Meanwhile, Daniel Jones of the Indianapolis Colts is listed to play despite a rough recent outing. Special teams shake-up: The Los Angeles Rams have promoted kicker Harrison Mevis (6-foot, 242-lb) ahead of their next game, replacing former starter Joshua Karty after several key misses.
